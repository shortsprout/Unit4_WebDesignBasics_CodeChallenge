Taking a stand against hunger one local garden at a time.


Healthy eating isn't just for the well-off. It should be for everyone. Unfortunately in some Dallas communities, fresh produce is scarce and family food budgets are small. We believe that healthy people live better lives, and that healthy eating should be accessible for everyone, regardless of income level. This is why the LemonAid Stand works to unite local growers and community partners to provide fresh local produce to the needy of Dallas County.
